# Commerce Replatforming Pattern

Pattern ID: TP-002

Version: 1.0

## 1. Pattern Overview

### 1.1 Pattern Name
Commerce Replatforming

### 1.2 Objective
Replace or modernize an existing commerce platform to improve agility, scalability, customer experience and maintainability.

### 1.3 Business Context
- Digital commerce transformation
- Legacy commerce platform
- Omnichannel strategy
- International expansion

### 1.4 Typical Trigger Events
- End of vendor support
- High maintenance costs
- Slow time-to-market
- Platform scalability issues

### 1.5 Expected Outcomes
- Faster business agility
- Reduced technical debt
- Improved customer experience
