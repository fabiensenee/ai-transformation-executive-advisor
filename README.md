# AI Transformation Executive Advisor

> An AI-powered decision support system for diagnosing, arbitrating, and steering large-scale business and technology transformations.

## Overview

AI Transformation Executive Advisor is an experiment in codifying executive transformation expertise into a structured, repeatable, and scalable advisory system.

The objective is not to replace transformation leaders.
The objective is to augment their ability to diagnose complex situations, identify root causes, evaluate strategic options, anticipate execution risks, and design actionable transformation roadmaps.

## Vision

Build a virtual transformation advisor capable of supporting leaders through critical transformation moments:

- Program Recovery
- Digital Transformation
- Commerce Replatforming
- Legacy Modernization
- Cloud Migration
- AI-First Transformation
- Post-Merger Integration
- Operating Model Redesign

## Core Capabilities

### Executive Diagnosis
- Separate symptoms from root causes
- Assess Strategy, Governance, Organization, Technology, and Delivery

### Strategic Arbitration
- Generate and evaluate alternative scenarios
- Compare value, cost, timeline, risk, and complexity

### Executive Recommendation
- Provide a recommended path
- Explain assumptions and trade-offs

### Transformation Roadmap
- 90-day plans
- Governance models
- Success metrics
- Risk mitigation actions
