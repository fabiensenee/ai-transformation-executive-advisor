# Program Recovery Pattern

Pattern ID: TP-001
Version: 1.0

# 1. Pattern Overview
## 1.1 Pattern Name
Program Recovery

## 1.2 Objective
Restore control, predictability, stakeholder confidence and business value realization.

## 1.3 Business Context
- Large-scale transformation program
- Multi-year initiative
- Multiple delivery partners

## 1.4 Typical Trigger Events
- Major delays
- Budget overruns
- Stakeholder dissatisfaction

## 1.5 Expected Outcomes
- Stabilized delivery
- Improved governance
- Restored confidence

# 2. Typical Symptoms
## 2.1 Business Symptoms
- Benefits not materializing

## 2.2 Governance Symptoms
- Slow decision making

## 2.3 Organization Symptoms
- Business versus IT tensions

## 2.4 Technology Symptoms
- Technical debt accumulation

## 2.5 Delivery Symptoms
- Milestone slippage
- Budget overruns
